Analog Engineer
Job Description
Analog Engineer in Assembly and Test Technology Development organization drives high speed IO (signal integrity) and power delivery related technology development and design of packages and interconnects for future products on Intel's roadmap. The main responsibility is to develop necessary methodology and metrology core competencies in modeling and lab work to support the aforementioned efforts. Defines overall electrical performance specification and realizes technology certification through extensive design, analysis and validation activities. Conducts and supports research through internal and external programs on fundamental areas pertinent to circuits, packages, and interconnects. Collaborates with other engineers in the team and helps establish a team atmosphere promoting growth of core competency areas.

This is an entry level position and will be compensated accordingly.


Qualifications


You must possess the below minimum qualifications to be initially considered for this position. Preferred qualifications are in addition to the minimum requirements and are considered a plus factor in identifying top candidates. Experience listed below would be obtained through a combination of your school work/classes/research and/or relevant previous job and/or internship experiences.?

Minimum Qualifications:

Posses a Ph.D. Degree focused on the areas of computational electromagnetics, RF, microwave engineering or photonics in Electrical and Computer Engineering, Computer Engineering, Electrical Engineering or related discipline.

Preferred Qualifications:

Demonstrate experience in packaging, interconnects, applied electromagnetics, RF design, and high speed circuit design.
Demonstrate experience in circuit analysis and signal & power integrity tools such as SPICE, HFSS and ADS.
Demonstrate a solid understanding of circuit theory, computational electromagnetics, transmission line fundamentals, microwave engineering, optical devices/interconnect and signal processing.
Demonstrate familiarity with computer architecture and fundamental scripting skills with tool such as Matlab.
Demonstrate familiarity with lab equipment as PNA, TDR, spectrum analyzer, etc.
Familiarity with analog circuit design, package/silicon layout design ?and VLSI will be a plus.
